Quick Setup Guide

Sr. no Question Answer
1 How do I connect using 2.4G mode? Locate the 2.4G USB receiver → Plug the receiver → Switch Power Button On → Press Fn + 3
2 How do I connect via Bluetooth mode? Press and hold Fn + 1 or Fn + 2 for 3 seconds → BT5.0 KB or BT3.0 KB
3 How do I Switch between paired devices Tap Fn + 1 or Fn + 2

Features and Customization

Sr. no Question Answer
1 How do I turn the RGB lighting on or off? Press FN + Backspace
2 How do I cycle through the different light animations? Press FN + Enter
3 Can I set the keyboard to a single static color? Press FN + |\
4 How do I change the brightness of the lights? Press FN + Up Arrow (↑) / Down Arrow (↓)
5 How do I change the speed of the "Breathing" or moving effects? Press FN + Right Arrow (→) / Left Arrow (←)
6 What information does the display screen show? Connectivity Status (2.4 Ghz, BT1, BT2)

Power Management

Sr. no Question Answer
1 What type of battery does the Visage keyboard use? 2000mAh Lithium-ion battery
2 How do I charge the keyboard? Connect the Type-C end to the keyboard and the USB-A end to a PC port or a standard 5V adapter.
3 How long will the battery last on a single charge? Approximately 100 working hours.
4 How does the "Power Saving" feature work? The keyboard is idle for a 90 sec period, the RGB lighting and display will dim or turn off. Simply tap any key to wake it up instantly.
