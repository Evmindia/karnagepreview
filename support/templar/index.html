TEMPLAR SUPPORT

Expert guides and technical specifications for the Templar Tri-Mode Gaming Mouse.

Product Overview

Sr. no Question Answer
1 What’s in the box?
  • 1 x Templar Tri-Mode Gaming Mouse
  • 1 x 2.4GHz Wireless USB Receiver
  • 1 x Braided Type-C Detachable Cable
  • 1 x Set of Extra Techflon Skates (0.6mm)
  • User Manual
2 What is the Templar Tri-Mode Gaming Mouse? The Templar is a Tri-mode Lightweight RGB gaming mouse that bridges the gap between professional performance and everyday portability. Weighing in at an incredible 57g (±3g), it is designed for gamers who require "flick" precision and minimal wrist fatigue.
3 What are the key features of the Templar?
  • 16,000 DPI Adjustable Sensor
  • 1000Hz polling rate
  • Tri-Mode Connectivity (Wired, 2.4GHz Wireless, and Bluetooth)
  • Huano Switches
  • Long-lasting 500mAh battery

Quick Setup Guide

Sr. no Question Answer
1 How do I power ON the mouse?
  • Switch the mode selector at the bottom of the mouse to 2.4GHz or Bluetooth to power it on.
  • The mouse powers on automatically in wired mode when connected via the Type-C cable.
2 How do I connect the mouse?
  • Wired: Plug the Type-C cable into the mouse and PC.
  • 2.4GHz Wireless: Insert the nano receiver into a USB port and switch to 2.4GHz mode.
  • Bluetooth: Switch to Bluetooth mode and pair with the device name.
3 Do I need to install drivers? Basic functionality works without drivers. To customize DPI, buttons, RGB lighting, polling rate, and profiles, installing the Karnage Gaming Mouse software is recommended.
4 How do I reset the mouse? Switch the mouse off, wait a few seconds, then power it back on. For persistent issues, reconnect the mouse using wired mode and reconfigure settings via software.

Features and Customization

Sr. no Question Answer
1 How can I adjust DPI settings? DPI can be adjusted through the software from 100 to 16,000 DPI. Default DPI steps include 1200, 2400, 3200, 6400, 8000 and 12,000.
2 What are the programmable buttons used for? The Templar features 6 programmable keys that can be remapped via software for macros, shortcuts, or in-game actions to enhance gameplay efficiency.
3 How do I control the RGB lighting? Press Forward Button + DPI Button to toggle modes manually, or customize extensively via the Karnage software.

Power Management

Sr. no Question Answer
1 What kind of battery does the Templar use? 500mAh Inbuilt Lithium Battery with up to 70 working hours on a single charge.
2 How do I charge the mouse? Connect the included Braided Type-C Detachable Cable to the PC and the front of the mouse. You can continue to use the mouse in Wired Mode while it charges.
